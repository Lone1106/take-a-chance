# Take a chance

Take a chance is a basic mutli step form to ask your crush out on a date.

***

## It features:
- a unclickable "no" button that moves away from your cursor
- Date & time selection
- Activity selection
- Sending you a confirmation mail via emailjs
- lots of stupid GIFs

***

## Don't forget about the .env
Take the ```.env.example``` and make a ```.env``` out of it. Fill in your emailjs details. Thats it
